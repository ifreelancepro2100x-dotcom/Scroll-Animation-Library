Turn Back the Clock, Naturally

As we age, our skin naturally produces less collagen and elastin—the essential proteins responsible for keeping skin firm, smooth, and youthful. Combined with sun exposure, weight fluctuations, and environmental stressors, this gradual collagen loss can lead to loose skin, fine lines, wrinkles, and reduced elasticity. At Strella Medical Aesthetics, we offer advanced collagen-stimulating skin rejuvenation treatments designed to tighten, lift, and refresh your skin without surgery.

Versatile Areas We Treat

One of the major advantages of non-surgical skin tightening is its versatility. Our innovative technologies can be performed on multiple areas of the body where collagen loss has reduced skin firmness.

What to Expect & Timeline

During Your Appointment

We prioritize your comfort and peace of mind. Standard appointments last between 30 and 60 minutes and consist of:

  • A thorough clinical evaluation of your treatment zone
  • Precise delivery of controlled thermal energy
  • A comfortable, gentle warming sensation
  • Post-treatment cool-down protocol

Gradual, Progressive Results

Because this treatment works by prompting natural collagen synthesis, results improve beautifully over time:

  • Subtle tightening may be noticed immediately
  • Early improvements typically show within several weeks
  • Peak tightening develops over two to three months
  • Periodic maintenance sessions can preserve long-term elasticity

Frequently Asked Questions

Is skin tightening painful?

Most patients describe the treatment as highly comfortable, experiencing only a mild warming sensation during the procedure.

How many treatments will I need?

The number of sessions depends entirely on your target skin condition, location, and desired outcome. We design a custom plan during your consultation.

Is there any downtime?

Most collagen-stimulating skin rejuvenation treatments require little to no downtime, allowing you to return to normal activities right after.

Who is a good candidate for skin tightening?

Adults experiencing mild to moderate skin laxity, reduced elasticity, or early signs of physical aging are excellent candidates.
